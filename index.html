About Us

Introduction

Jesus And Me (JAM) Ministry in Uganda is an establishment of 2024, by a group of youths with the vision of transforming the current generation through the true act of worship. The ministry therefore employs a variety of strategies to achieve her vision and objectives.

Background

It is a Biblical mandate that we worship God and Him alone. It is an act that is designed not only to bring honor to Him who deserves honor, but also to bring a spirit of obedience and submission to the worshippers. The JAM ministry was started to explore the true act of worship and share this with communities.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A transformed generation through the true act of worship.

Our Mission

To empower and equip the current generation to become transformed through the true act of worship.

Objectives

  • ✓

    To provide effective worship programs for the youth who have chosen to explore the true act of worship whereby they receive encouragement, support, peer accountability, mentorship from like-minded fellows.

  • ✓

    To provide mechanism for the youth to reach out to the fellow youth who may need to learn more about the true worship through scripture sharing, music, mentorship programs and fun as well.

  • ✓

    To provide a platform where the youth and other members of the community can be sensitized and empowered to develop Christ-like character, values and principles and base their lives on the truth.

  • ✓

    To introduce the current youth generation to exploration of a variety of culture of worship based on the truth for transformation.
